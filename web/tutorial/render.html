Rendering Animations

  1. To speed up the rendering process, change the start frame to 87. This will cause only frames 87 and later to be rendered.
  2. To render your animation, click on the Render (camera) icon in the far right panel.
  3. Press the "Animation" button, and wait.

Once your animation has finished rendering, click the play button to view it at full speed.
